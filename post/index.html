About me

I’m a three-years PhD student supervised by Prof. Wei Feng in the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University (TJU). I was graduated from Suzhou University of Science and Technology as a master at 2018 advised bf Prof. Fuyuan Hu. My major is Computer Application Technology, and my research focuses on Lifelong Learning and Computer Vision. At TJU, my research interests at present mainly focus on the Lifelong Learning (Also known as Continual Learning or Incremental Learning), which aims to continually learn new knowledge from a sequence of tasks over a lifelong time. However, current lifelong learning can not support the practical demands in the real world. Therefore, my main research goal is to make the lifelong learning more general in more scenarios. I also have good Computer Vision background and have studied and published on the areas including Multi-label image classification, Image Captioning, Visual Question Answering and Visual Grounding.

I am looking for a postdoct researcher position for now, and any suggestion is ok to my mailbox.
